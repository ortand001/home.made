Who we are

At home.made, we believe your build should be as unique as you are. We combine the strength and precision of light steel frame construction with fully customizable designs, so every home we build is truly one of a kind.

Specialising in small-space pod units, we create unique solutions tailored to your needs, stripping away the inefficiencies of traditional construction.

  • Light steel framing means faster build times and less waste
  • Every component is precision-engineered to fit together perfectly
  • Factory production runs concurrent with on-site groundworks
  • Structures designed to stand the test of time

Why home.made

Built for where
others cannot.

Our approach strips away the inefficiencies of traditional construction. Light steel framing means faster build times, less waste, and structures that stand the test of time.

Faster Build Times
Factory production runs concurrently with on-site groundworks, dramatically reducing your project timeline and weather exposure.
Terrain Versatility
Our unique foundation design allows building on slopes, uneven territory, and eco-sensitive sites where traditional construction cannot go.
Sustainable and Green
Precision engineering minimises material waste. Units are designed to be relocated, reconfigured, or expanded as your needs evolve.
Future-Proof
Grow, relocate, or reconfigure. Finishes scale up or down with your budget, so your home adapts as your life changes.

How it works

From concept
to handover.

01
Design
Select your modules from our Design menu: bedroom, bath, kitchen, living, deck. Choose layouts, cladding, roofing, flooring, and premium extras.
02
Engineer
Every unit is precision-engineered to your specification. Our structural team certifies each component before production begins.
03
Build
Factory production of your units runs concurrently with on-site groundworks, dramatically reducing your total project timeline.
04
Handover
Units are transported to site, assembled, finished, and handed over. A transparent, streamlined process from first sketch to final key.
